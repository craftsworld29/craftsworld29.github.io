About Us

Craftsworld29 is a home based brand established in January 2019, started simply for the love of craft.At present craftworld29 is a neophyte local store, however aims to have a global reach someday, with your support and amity.

Each piece we make is specially handcrafted with love and precision keeping your requirement and style in mind; to bring you the most beautiful accessories at affordable prices.

From bows, boutonnieres, earrings, crocheted works to tiaras. we provide the choicest collection of handmade accessories to suit your every occasion, we have it all!!! So hurry up and grab your personalized accessories at affordable rates!

For all the little ones out there, we also make knitted items to meet your style and comfort requirement!

We hope you enjoy shopping with us.
